How To Play
How many words can you make from 7 letters shown?
- Words must contain at least 4 letters.
- Words must include the center letter.
- Letters can be used more than once.
- Our words list does not include curses or words that are obscure.
Every word earns points.
- 4-letter words are worth 1 point.
- Longer words earn 1 point for each letter.
- Panagram - words that use all 7 letters -- earn an
additional bonus of 7 points. There is at least one Panagram per
puzzle.
New puzzles generate at midnight.
